Popular Places in the World:

  • Taj Mahal, India

    The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra. It is one of the most beautiful buildings in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • Eiffel Tower, Paris, France

    The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is one of the most famous landmarks in the world.

  • Machu Picchu, Peru

    Machu Picchu is an ancient Incan citadel located in the Andes Mountains of Peru. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular tourist destination.

  • Great Barrier Reef, Australia

    The Great Barrier Reef is the world's largest coral reef system located off the coast of Queensland, Australia. It is renowned for its biodiversity and marine life.

  • Grand Canyon, USA

    The Grand Canyon is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River in Arizona, USA. It is one of the most iconic natural landmarks in the United States.

  • Pyramids of Giza, Egypt

    The Pyramids of Giza are ancient Egyptian pyramids located on the Giza Plateau, outside Cairo. They are among the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

  • Colosseum, Rome, Italy

    The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater in Rome, Italy. It is one of the greatest works of Roman engineering and architecture and a symbol of ancient Rome's grandeur.

  • Angkor Wat, Cambodia

    Angkor Wat is a temple complex in Cambodia and the largest religious monument in the world. It was originally constructed as a Hindu temple for the Khmer Empire.
